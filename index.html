About me

My name is Ali Mahmoudi Derakhsh. I was born in 1983 and I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Engineering with a focus on Artificial Intelligence and Robotics from the Islamic Azad University, Science and Research Branch. I also have a Master’s degree in Software Design and Production from Azad University of Tehran West, and a Bachelor's degree in Software Technology Engineering from University of Science and Culture.

Over the past 15 years, I’ve worked on a wide range of software projects, from user management systems and legal platforms to business intelligence, ESB, and BPMS systems. I’ve had the opportunity to lead several of these projects as a software project manager, while also teaching at university level and supervising students' theses.

My main areas of expertise include natural language processing, machine vision, machine learning, deep learning, statistical analysis, and smart system design. I enjoy working with Python and its data science ecosystem—especially tools like TensorFlow, PyTorch, scikit-learn, and OpenCV. I also have experience with web development, databases, network infrastructure, and academic research.

In recent years, I’ve published several papers in areas such as optimization, deep learning, game theory, and blockchain applications. I’m passionate about both the academic and practical sides of AI, and I’m always looking for ways to bridge research with real-world solutions.
